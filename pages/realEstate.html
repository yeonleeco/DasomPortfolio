House Rent App

OVERVIEW

When people want to find the house list, they want simple layout and better
filtering. The goal of this particular project is to build user-friendly mobile
app including intuitive design and to help users finding what they want.

When finding a house in a listing, people want to compare information about the houses they have seen. Users want to compare different details such as price, amentities, floor plan, etc. to decide on a house carefully. The House Rent App main page shows the house history they saw recently and helps users make smarter decisions.

The most important point of the search page is that it contains
all of the filters entered by the user, and briefly shows the
simple but essential information.

Filtering page

House Rent App provides easy-following layouts to users with
an uncomplicated configuration. The wide picture and proper
font layout make the content easier to read at a glance.
The use of pictograms provides visualized information rather
than monotonous text, and is filled with simple but visual elements.

Users can see the list of saved apartment. We've put together a large layout
so they can easily compare the prices and photos of each list.
You can send messages in real time with the listing agent of the apartment
you are interested in. The currently selected property is shown at the top.
