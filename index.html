Dasom Kim is a Web Designer and UX/UI Designer based in Sandy, Utah.

I value at Web & UX/UI design which is Professional, Passionate, and Proactive. Balance, Unite, and Perfection is my Motive.

THIS IS DASOM KIM

To shortly introduce myself, I am a skilled and results-driven individual who is able to function well in fastpace and deadline-driven environments. I have had the opportunities to create various designs such as magazine, book cover, icon set, app layout (UI Design), etc., and I have done my best to make all designs creative and innovative.

I am adept at working with all Adobe software tools, Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign, XD, and Animate. On top of my experience, I worked as a UI Designer in the team which creates outdoor apps, and created an app design prototype to provide them with a design layout.
